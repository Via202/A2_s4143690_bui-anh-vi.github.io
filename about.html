About Me

Hi, I'm Vi, a Digital Media student at RMIT University.

I'm interested in interactive web design, 2D and 3D graphics, and I have a passion for visual storytelling.

I have experience in collaborative projects and have previously designed a brand identity website for Unilever products.

My main goal is to bring ideas to life through digital platforms, from scratch to execution.

HTML / CSS / Javascript
